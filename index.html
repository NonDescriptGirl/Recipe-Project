Stuffed Pepper Explosion

This recipie is great for a weeknight when you want dinner on the table in under 45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup lentils
  • 1½ cups rice
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup onion, chopped
  • ½ ground beef, browned
  • 2 green bell peppers, chopped
  • 1 (14.5 oz.) canned diced tomatoes
  • 6 oz. cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 Tbsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp red pepper flakes
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Recipe

  1. Prepare rice and lentils according to their package directions.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°.
  3. In a medium pan, sautè onion in olive oil until translucent.
  4. Add cooked ground beef and stir until warm.
  5. Add bell peppers to pan, turn heat down to medium and cover, stirring occasionaly.
  6. When pepper has softened, add spices, tomatoes, cooked lentils, and salt/pepper to taste. Continue cooking a few additional minutes to blend the flavors.
  7. In a large bowl combine the beef and vegetable mixture, rice and 1 cup of cheese, mix thoroughly.
  8. Spoon into a greased 9" x 13" casserole dish, covering with foil.
  9. Cook for 25 minutes. Remove from oven, remove foil carefully and add remaining cheese.
  10. Place back into oven to broil 2-3 minutes to gently crisp cheese.
